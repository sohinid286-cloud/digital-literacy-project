
STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST


*Use strong, unique passwords 🔐*
  Don’t reuse the same password across apps (Instagram, Gmail, college portals). Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols—or a password manager.

•⁠  ⁠*Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA)*
  Always turn on 2FA for email, banking, and social media. It adds an extra layer even if your password is leaked.

•⁠  ⁠*Think before you post 📱*
  Avoid sharing personal details like your address, phone number, college ID, or daily routine publicly.

•⁠  ⁠*Don’t trust unknown links or messages*
  If you receive a random “offer”, internship link, or prize message—even from a known contact—verify before clicking.

•⁠  ⁠*UPI Safety Tip 1 💸: Never approve unknown payment requests*
  Apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm will never ask you to “receive money” by entering a PIN. If someone asks, it’s a scam.

•⁠  ⁠*UPI Safety Tip 2 💸: Double-check QR codes and IDs*
  Before paying, confirm the name shown on the app. Scammers often use fake QR codes in PGs, cafés, or OLX deals.

•⁠  ⁠*Be careful on public Wi-Fi 🌐*
  Avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts on free Wi-Fi in cafés or hostels. Use mobile data or a VPN instead.

•⁠  ⁠*Limit app permissions*
  Don’t allow unnecessary access to your contacts, camera, or location unless it’s essential.

•⁠  ⁠*Speak up if you’re targeted*
  If you face cyberbullying or harassment, don’t stay silent—tell a trusted friend, senior, or college authority.

•⁠  ⁠*Report cybercrime immediately 🚨*

  * National Cyber Crime Portal: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
  * Helpline: *1930*
    Quick reporting can help prevent financial loss and catch offenders early.
